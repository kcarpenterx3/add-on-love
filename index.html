Why South Korea?

South Korea, home of many mountains

South Korea is a very mountainous area because it's been built on very rocky terrian. 70% of the country is mountainous and as of 2022 it had a minimum population of 51 million people.
There are 21 national parks, but the only areas of true wilderness left are the mountain forests. Most of its wealth comes from manufacturing and service industries, such as banking. It exports ships, cars, computers, and other electronic items.
